# 📖 The Lexicon Folio

**A beautiful, vintage-style dictionary in your browser.**

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)

---

## ✨ Features

- **📚 260,000+ words** – Powered by the Open Dictionary dataset.
- **📖 Book-like design** – Parchment background, elegant typography, two‑column layout.
- **🔍 Smart search** – Find any word instantly.
- **📑 A–Z browsing** – Click any letter on the thumb‑index to jump to that section.
- **🕯️ Lamplight mode** – Warm, dimmed reading experience for the eyes.
- **🎲 Random word spinner** – Discover new words by chance.
- **🔗 Lexical neighbours** – See the words before and after the current entry.
- **⚡ Lightweight & offline‑ready** – Data is cached after first load.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).
